Density

Dense high-rise buildings in Hong Kong are always overwhelming and impressing. Conceptualizing from the vertical and density, the swimming centre is similar to its environment to some extent but with strong contrast. The net of the slim and dense columns forms a forest-like space which would moderate the restless chaos from the city, allowing people to find their own peace and serenity.
