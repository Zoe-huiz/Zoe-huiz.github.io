Lego

Housing issue is one of the most controversial topics in Hong Kong. When asked to propose a prototype of a new community to young people, we found that there is great potential to revitalize back alleys in the downtown areas, which are nowadays full of garbage and cause a huge waste of city space. Furthermore, we also tried to occupy the space above existing buildings and to introduce a mega structure to draw the public’s attention to how young people’s living space has been sacrificed because of the life pressure and the great living cost of the modern society.

With the crane at the top and a panel system inspired by LEGO, the whole community can be built by young people who wish to live there, and it can be expanded organically and more and more people can move in.

Parasite Structure

The parasite structure is attached on the existing building. The entrance to the community is inside the back alleys and connect with the pedstrains. The open system and self-as-sembled modular units allow the structure to expand with high-efficiency.
