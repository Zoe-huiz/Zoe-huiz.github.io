From spiritual garden

From organic farmland

Section

As a transition located between the farm land and the spiritual garden, the learning centre makes layers in the process of cooking in an architectural way. People get raw material from the farmland, process it in the kitchen, share and enjoy it in the hall and finally they rest and reflect themselves when sitting in the corridor facing the spiritual garden. Different activities in the different layers of the learning centre try to push people to reflect and respect in the process of learning cooking.

The highlight of the project is experienced through the layers as it frames certain experience at different moments. These layers respond to the surrounding context with varying levels of activity and interaction.
